In the interest of fostering an open and welcoming community, we as contributors and maintainers of the bobsoapwins/security-template project pledge to make participation in our project and our community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of experience level, gender, gender identity and expression, sexual orientation, disability, physical appearance, body size, race, ethnicity, age, religion, or political affiliation.

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:
- Demonstrating empathy and kindness toward other people.
- Being respectful in tone and language in all communications (issues, PRs, code comments, discussions).
- Listening and seeking to understand before responding.
- Giving and receiving constructive feedback in a professional manner.
- Acknowledging and correcting mistakes when they occur.

Examples of unacceptable behavior:
- Harassment, intimidation, or discrimination in any form.
- Abusive, derogatory, or demeaning language or imagery.
- Publishing other people’s private information (doxxing).
- Sustained disruption of project discussions or deliberate sabotage of the project.
- Threats of violence, or encouragement of self-harm.

This Code of Conduct applies to all spaces managed by the bobsoapwins/security-template project, including:
- GitHub issues, pull requests, and comments
- Project discussions and chats (linked from the repository)
- Events, meetings, and any official or community activities related to the project

If you experience or witness behavior that violates this Code of Conduct, please report it as soon as possible. You can report in one of the following ways:
- Open a private issue in this repository labelled “code-of-conduct” (if you prefer confidentiality, mention it in the report).
  - https://github.com/bobsoapwins/security-template/issues/new
- Send a private email to the project maintainers: [MAINTAINER_EMAIL_HERE]

When reporting, please include:
- A description of the incident with date/time and location (issue, PR, chat room, etc.)
- Any relevant usernames and links
- Screenshots or copies of messages, if available
- Whether you want the report to be kept confidential

If you do not want the report to be visible to the public, explicitly request confidentiality in your report.

All reports will be reviewed and investigated by the project maintainers or appointed Code of Conduct officers. Possible outcomes include:
- A private warning issued to the offender
- A request for corrective action (apology, retraction)
- Temporary or permanent suspension from project spaces (blocking from repository interactions)
- Escalation to platform administrators when appropriate

We will aim to acknowledge receipt of a report within 5 business days and provide an estimated timeline for resolution. The maintainers reserve the right to take any action they deem necessary to preserve the safety and well-being of the community.

If you are subject to an enforcement action and believe it to be unjust, you may appeal by contacting the maintainers at [MAINTAINER_EMAIL_HERE] with further context. Appeals will be reviewed by an independent maintainer or appointed reviewer when possible.

This Code of Conduct is adapted for the bobsoapwins/security-template project. Community principles are inspired by commonly used open source codes of conduct.
